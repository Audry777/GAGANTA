DEAN

February 24, 2025

Dean sat in the sterile, cold room, her breath shallow, her heart pounding like a drum in her chest. The faint hum of machinery filled the air, and the glow of neon lights outside the window cast eerie shadows on the walls. She clenched her fists, her nails digging into her palms, as the doctors prepared her for the procedure. This was it—the moment she’d both feared and longed for. The cybernetic arm lay on the table beside her, sleek and metallic, a masterpiece of technology that promised to restore what she had lost. But it wasn’t just an arm; it was a piece of her future, a fragment of her identity, and a reminder of the accident that had shattered her world. ‍
